Apple I

The Apple I was the first afforable consumers computer on the market. It did not come with a case, so the buyer would have to make one.

Apple II

The Apple II was the 2nd computer that Apple made, it was their first computer to come with a case, but the buyer would still have to buy a monitor.

Macintosh

Ah, yes, now the Macintosh. This is what started it all. This peice of history cost alot back in its launch in 1984. It set the ground rule for Apple.

iMac G3

This was the first iMac, released in the year 1998. It started Apples nice design for their products.

iMac G4

This is the iMac G4, well-known as the desk lamp iMac. It was a step up from the G3, with a G4 processor. It came with a 15'' model, a 17'', and a 20'' inch model, all with different specs.
